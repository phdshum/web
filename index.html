I am a Chartered Engineer and a graduate of Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). My Ph.D. supervisor was the former department head, Professor Kwong-Sak, Leung and my research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Evolutionary Computation, Data Mining and Neural Network.

         I am also a full member of various professional bodies, like ACM, IEEE, British Computer Society and Hong Kong Computer Society; and got numerous qualifications including Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) and Project Management Professional (PMP).

PhD Thesis:
My thesis title is Discovering acyclic dependency relationships by Evolutionary Computation, which has 10 chapters, 261 pages (single line spacing) and the ISBN is 9780549400998. The thesis described 5 novel algorithms and 2 novel techniques; and is available in CUHK's library, The Open University of Hong Kong's library and Proquest.

Biography:
By invitations, my biography was published in several Marquis Who's Who series.
